## Conclusion

**Current Coverage: ~20% of industry standard**

To match premium templates on CodeCanyon/ThemeForest, we need:
- **50 more atoms** (form controls, feedback, display)
- **35 more molecules** (cards, navigation, lists)
- **27 more organisms** (sections, complex components)
- **7 more templates** (different use cases)

**Estimated Timeline:** 12-16 weeks for full coverage at current pace

**Priority:** Start with atoms (forms + feedback) as they're the building blocks for everything else.
